ADiscordBot Command List

@aDiscordBot taskkill - Kills the bot's main program. [Bot admin only command]

@aDiscordBot say [text] - The bot will say whatever you wrote.

@aDiscordBot purge [amount] - The bot will delete said amount of messages (This command is currently still a work in progress, its pretty slow). [Server admin only command]

@aDiscordBot cursedimage - The bot will send a random cursed image.
